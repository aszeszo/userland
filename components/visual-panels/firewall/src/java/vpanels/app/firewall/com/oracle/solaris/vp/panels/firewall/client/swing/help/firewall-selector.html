Firewall Policy Selection

The Firewall Policy selector is located on the left side of the Service Firewall panel. From this list you can:

  • Select to view or edit the system-wide firewall policy.

  • Select a firewall policy for a specific service.

  • Add a firewall policy for a specific service from the displayed list once the Add service policy button is selected.

  • Delete a service specific policy by selecting the appropriate entry from the selector and clicking the Delete button. A popup asks you to verify your selection before the entry is deleted.
